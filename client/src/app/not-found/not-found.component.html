CPSAC has a new site! We are pleased to share with everyone the news that CPSAC has developed a brand new website that now includes a built in "Find-A-Tech" map. The new site looks amazing and has the support needed to take on the maintenance for this important service.

It's been a privilege over the years to help support all of the hardworking techs and are fully confident the integrated map is the right way to go. Please go HERE and take a look. If you are a CPSAC certified tech you can sign up for the new map by following these steps: 1. Click Member Login at the top right. 2. Scroll down and click Your Password. 3. Enter the username you have on file with CPSAC OR the email address where you receive CPSAC newsletters. 4. Check your e-mail for the confirmation link to reset your password (this may take up to 15 minutes). 5. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password. 6. In the top right corner you will see Members on the menu bar. Click it to open the members only menu. If you run into problems or have any questions you can reach out for help by email.
